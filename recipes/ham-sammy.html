Ham Sandwich

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4-6 slices of ham
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Spread mayonnaise and mustard on one slice of bread.
  2. Layer the ham, lettuce, and tomato on the bread.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Top with the second slice of bread.
  5. Cut in half and serve.
