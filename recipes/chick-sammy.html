Chicken Sandwich

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Brioche Bun
  • Grilled chicken breast
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Spread mayonnaise and mustard on bottumn bun.
  2. Layer the grilled chicken breast, lettuce, and tomato on the bread.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Top with the top bun.
  5. Cut in half and serve.
